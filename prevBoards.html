Does Swades arrange airport pick-up for new students?

Swades can share links from where reasonable pick-up can be arranged.

Incase a student volunteer is available on the date and time of your arrival, a pick-up can be arranged

What are amongst the first things that I must do on arrival at SCU?

Following are the things that need to be done:
  • Register yourself at International Student Services(ISS) and get a new I-20 issued
  • Get your ID card made from the ID card office at Benson Memorial
  • Complete the fees payment procedure so that your ID card is activated
  • Get the credentials for the WiFi so that you can access the Internet on campus.
  • Meet the graduate adviser assigned to you and plan your course of study
  • Make sure the Medical Insurance formalities are completed before deadline
  • Attend the ISS orientation where all the details would be explained to you

Do I have to bring my International Driving License (IDP) / Indian License?

There is no harm in getting your International Driving License (IDP) or Indian License. You can use your IDP and drive for 1 year in the US and you can use your Indian License and drive for 6 months (Please check the official DMV website for the latest news on these durations) Having your IDP or Indian License, you can use them to get Zipcar membership from which you can rent a car and go grocery shopping or nearby places if you wish to.

When can I start my internship? When am I eligible for an internship?

  • On F-1 Visa, you will have to do 3 consecutive quarters of full-time course-work to be eligible (8 units per quarter for engineering and 9 unit per quarter for MSIS/MBA), and then you will be eligible for doing an internship.

  • You can work 40hrs/week during the summer quarter and 20hrs/week during any other quarter when you have course-work.

Who are Advisor, what do they do?

Advisors are professors who are here to help you with your course work and what courses to take. For few majors (eg: CS), SCU assigns you an advisor and for other majors (eg: EE), you can choose your advisor after coming to US within a month.
Advisors are important for approving your Program of Studies.
Please always stay in touch with your advisors.

Should I get a Laptop from India or should I buy a laptop on coming to the US?

If you already own a laptop in India, and plan to use that in the future, get it with you. If you are planning to buy a new one, buy one from the US.
There are 2 reasons for this:

  • You get an insurance from US rather than India.
  • Laptops are much cheaper in the US than in India.
